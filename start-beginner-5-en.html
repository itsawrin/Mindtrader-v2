What are Altcoins and Shitcoins?

Altcoin: Any cryptocurrency other than Bitcoin. Some of the most well-known ones include:

  • Ethereum (ETH) – (Utility / Platform Token)
  • Solana (SOL) – (Utility Token)
  • Cardano (ADA) – (Utility + Governance Token)

Shitcoin: A crypto with no clear purpose, project, or utility — often made for fun or pump-and-dump (artificially raising the price through hype and selling at the peak). Examples:

  • Pepe Coin – (Meme token with no real-world use)
  • SafeMoon – (Hyped token that later lost most of its value)

Warning: Some shitcoins can be scam projects that may wipe out your money. Investing heavily in them is like gambling and not recommended.

Key Difference: Altcoins usually solve real problems, have a dev team, and long-term goals. Shitcoins are mostly useless and driven by hype.
