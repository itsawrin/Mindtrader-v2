Main Categories of Cryptocurrencies + Examples

In the crypto world, coins and tokens are generally grouped into a few major categories:

  • Stablecoins: Like USDT and USDC, these are pegged to a stable asset like the US dollar and have low volatility.
  • Payment Coins: Like Bitcoin (BTC) and Litecoin (LTC), designed for sending, receiving, and buying stuff.
  • Utility Tokens: Like BNB (Binance Coin) and Chainlink (LINK), provide access to platform services.
  • Governance Tokens: Like Uniswap (UNI) and Aave (AAVE), allow users to vote on protocol decisions.
  • NFTs & Digital Assets: Like Decentraland (MANA) and The Sandbox (SAND), represent unique items or land.
  • Security Tokens: Like INX and tZERO, represent shares or real-world financial assets.

There may be more categories in the future, but these six are the most fundamental right now.
