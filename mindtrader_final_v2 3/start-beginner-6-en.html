What is a Whitepaper and Project Team?

Whitepaper is an official document that explains what a crypto project is about, what problem it solves, and how it works.

For example, Ethereum has a whitepaper written by Vitalik Buterin, explaining the idea of smart contracts.

The team includes developers, managers, and advisors who build and maintain the project. Cardano was founded by Charles Hoskinson, one of Ethereum’s co-founders.

How to check if a project is legit:

  • Look for the whitepaper on the official website (usually on the homepage or About section).
  • Websites like CoinMarketCap and CoinGecko show whitepaper links and team details.
  • If the team is hidden or photos look fake, that’s a red flag!
